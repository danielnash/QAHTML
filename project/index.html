New York
New York is a city and state on the east coast of the United States of America.
Here's some basic facts about the city:
  • New York city is the most populous city in the whole of the USA with a 2015 population of over 8.5M!
  • New York city is also the USA's most densely populated city
  • It's been the home of many successful TV shows, such as Friends, Saturday Night Live and news providers such as CNN and Fox
  • It's an extremely linguistically diverse city, with its residents speaking over 800 languages
